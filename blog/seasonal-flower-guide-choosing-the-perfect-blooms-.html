Seasonal Flower Guide: Choosing the Perfect Blooms for Every Month

A comprehensive guide to selecting and caring for seasonal flowers in Calgary's unique climate

Spring (March - May)

As Calgary emerges from winter, early spring brings forth a stunning array of blooms. Tulips lead the charge, offering a rainbow of colors from deep purples to sunny yellows. Daffodils and narcissi follow closely behind, their cheerful faces brightening gardens and arrangements alike. Late spring introduces peonies, whose lush, multi-petaled blooms have become increasingly popular for both garden plantings and special occasions.

Spring Care Tips:

  • Keep bulb flowers in cool locations to extend their life
  • Change water every 2-3 days for maximum freshness
  • Remove foliage below the waterline to prevent bacterial growth

Summer (June - August)

Summer brings an explosion of color and variety to Calgary's flower scene. Garden roses reach their peak, offering countless varieties and fragrances. Hydrangeas provide substantial blooms perfect for statement arrangements, while delicate wildflowers add whimsical charm to any bouquet. Sunflowers, lilies, and dahlias thrive in our warm summer months, each bringing unique characteristics to seasonal arrangements.

Popular Summer Varieties:

  • David Austin Roses
  • Lisianthus
  • Delphinium
  • Cosmos

Fall (September - November)

Autumn brings rich, warm tones to our floral palette. Chrysanthemums dominate the season with their variety of shapes and colors. Japanese anemones offer delicate beauty, while ornamental grasses add texture and movement to arrangements. This is also the perfect time for dried flower arrangements, incorporating wheat, preserved leaves, and seasonal berries.

Winter (December - February)

Winter needn't mean an end to beautiful blooms. Forced bulbs like amaryllis and paperwhites bring life to indoor spaces. Holiday arrangements featuring evergreen boughs, holly, and winter berries create festive atmosphere. Greenhouse-grown varieties ensure year-round access to classic favorites like roses and carnations.

Year-Round Care Essentials:

  • Always use clean, sharp scissors when cutting stems
  • Remove leaves that would sit below the waterline
  • Use flower food provided by your florist
  • Keep arrangements away from direct sunlight and heat sources

Understanding seasonal availability helps in creating stunning arrangements year-round. Working with your local Calgary florist ensures access to the freshest seasonal blooms and expert advice on selection and care. Whether you're planning a special event or simply brightening your home, choosing seasonal flowers guarantees the best quality and value.
