About Bloom & Co.

Founded in 2010 by master florist Sarah bloom, Bloom & Co. began as a small neighborhood flower shop in the heart of Calgary. What started as a passion for creating beautiful arrangements has grown into Calgary's premier floral design studio, serving thousands of happy customers across the city.

Our mission is to bring joy and beauty to every occasion through the artistry of fresh flowers. We believe in sustainable practices, supporting local growers, and creating moments of delight for our customers. Our core values of creativity, quality, and exceptional service guide everything we do.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your delivery area?

We deliver throughout Calgary and surrounding areas including Airdrie, Chestermere, and Cochrane. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2 PM within Calgary city limits.

How long will my flowers last?

With proper care, our arrangements typically last 7-10 days. We provide detailed care instructions with every delivery and offer a 7-day freshness guarantee.

Do you offer custom arrangements?

Yes! We love creating custom arrangements. Contact our design team to discuss your vision, preferences, and budget. We're happy to accommodate special requests.

What are your payment options?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payments including Apple Pay and Google Pay. Corporate accounts can be set up with net-30 terms.

Do you offer wedding and event services?

Yes, we specialize in weddings and events of all sizes. Our event team provides full-service floral design, including consultation, setup, and teardown. Contact us for a custom quote.
