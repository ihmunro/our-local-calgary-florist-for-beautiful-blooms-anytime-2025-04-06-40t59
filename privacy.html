Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Bloom & Co., we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name and contact information
  • Billing and delivery addresses
  • Payment information
  • Order history and preferences

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Communicate with you about your orders
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Improve our products and services

Cookie Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our website and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amounts of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.
