Seasonal Flower Guide: Choosing the Perfect Blooms for Every Month

April 6, 2025

Discover the perfect flowers for every season in Calgary with this comprehensive guide. Spring welcomes tulips, daffodils, and luxurious peonies, while summer explodes with garden roses, hydrangeas, and vibrant wildflowers. Fall brings rich-toned chrysanthemums and Japanese anemones, perfect for creating warm, seasonal arrangements. Even winter offers beautiful options with forced bulbs like amaryllis and festive holiday arrangements featuring evergreens and berries. Learn essential care tips for maintaining fresh blooms year-round, from proper stem cutting to water maintenance. Whether planning an event or brightening your home, understanding seasonal availability ensures the best quality flowers throughout the year.
